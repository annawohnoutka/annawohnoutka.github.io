03 : About me

Hi, I'm Anna

Just me, myself and I, exploring beautiful Western Washington and beyond.

Academics

I am a senior at Western Washington University in Fairhaven College, where I have gone through a rigorous process to design my own major. I am majoring in International Studies & Communications with a Focus in Marketing. My coursework has allowed me to examine systemic forces relating to sexism, racism, and homophobia and their impacts on modern day societies and negative effects legally, socially, and economically. I am specifically interested in women's liberation, workplace rights, and bodily autonomy. I have also studied the sociology of communication, power dynamics, and mass media. The final aspect of my major focuses on marketing, which I believe is about understanding and having the ability to connect with others. The marketing aspect of my major introduced me to ethics, globalization, domestic and international business law, and economic theory.

Work... or is it?

I have been a sailing instructor to people of all ages and abilities since I was 17. I have taught dinghies, keelboats, catamarans in institutions on Lake Washington, Lake Whatcom, and Bellingham Bay. I am currently moonlighting as a Sailing Instructor and finishing a Marketing and Communications Internship at the Community Boating Center, in Fairhaven Washington. :)

Passions

I am passionate about the maritime industry, outdoor recreation, social media marketing, outreach, event planning, and promotions, as well as connecting people to various communities and idea shareing platforms.
